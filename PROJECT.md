
# ADHD Compass

## Vision

ADHD Compass is a free, research-backed platform that helps people with ADHD understand, manage, and live better with ADHD.

The platform should be educational, practical, easy to navigate, and designed specifically for people with ADHD.

It should not replace professional medical advice.

---

# Target Users

Primary

- Teenagers with ADHD
- Adults with ADHD
- Students
- People who suspect they have ADHD

Secondary

- Parents
- Teachers
- Employers
- General public

---

## Medication Library

Each medication page should include:

- What it is
- How it works
- Positive effects
- Side effects
- Pros
- Cons
- Warnings
- Duration
- References

---

## ADHD Screening

Use validated screening questionnaires.

Explain clearly that screening tools are NOT diagnoses.

Recommend professional assessment when appropriate.

---

# Design Philosophy

The website should feel calm, modern, premium, and easy to use.

Inspired by:

- Apple
- Linear
- Notion

Avoid clutter.

Use plenty of whitespace.

Large readable typography.

Smooth but subtle animations.

Excellent responsiveness.

Dark Mode support.

Accessibility should follow WCAG 2.2 AA guidelines.
